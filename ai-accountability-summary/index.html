The Horizon Principle. What Buyers Are Trained to Detect.
Three cases now define buyer scrutiny. Post Office Horizon, not AI, but the governance failure that defines the doctrine. Information asymmetry plus blind institutional trust equals catastrophic harm at scale. Dutch SyRI, the first court ruling to halt an AI system (The Hague, Feb 2020) on fundamental rights grounds. DWP Universal Credit AI, active today, scoring around 1M claims a year. Bias by age, disability, and nationality disclosed via FOI. Evaluators are trained on all three. Suppliers must demonstrate they have learned the same lessons.

Three Cases Every Evaluator Now Knows
Governance Failure
Post Office Horizon
700+ wrongful convictions. The supplier concealed defects. The institution trusted outputs blindly. Not AI, but the doctrine it created underpins every AI governance question asked today.
"The system never lies." The culture buyers are trained to break in suppliers.
Court-Halted AI System
Dutch SyRI
An ML algorithm scored low-income communities for fraud risk. The Hague court halted it in Feb 2020, the first ever ruling to stop an AI system on ECHR Art. 8 grounds.
Hidden algorithmic harm violates fundamental rights, and will be litigated.
Active UK System · 2024
DWP Universal Credit AI
Scores around 1M claims a year. Internal analysis found bias by age, disability, and nationality. Disclosed via FOI, not proactively. Passive monitoring is not enough.
Proactive disclosure is now the expected standard, not reactive compliance.
